Milgam Cellular Parking (MCP) provides a comprehensive turnkey mobile payment parking solution under the brand Pango+ in Israel and Parking+ international to local government and parking lot owners

Our vision is to become the leading worldwide provider of Mobile Payment Parking Solutions

ON-STREET PARKING

Our solution offers pay by space and pay by zone together with efficient and highly reliable enforcement. Within very few years since its establishment. Our brand in Israel, Pango+ has become a dominant force in the mobile payment parking industry in Israel. Connected to almost 50 municipalities and processing over $50 million USD in annual parking fees.

Following a quick, one-time, registration process the driver can easily activate the parking using his Smartphone (iPhone and Android) with our location based application and with just one-click activation. As an Alternative, MCP provides two solutions for feature phones one is with single line SMS text message that includes the city name and zone. The second alternative is to activate the parking by dialing to an Interactive Voice Response (IVR) with a voice recognition feature which allows the driver to say the name of city and choose the zone

OFF-STREET PARKING

Our solution is integrated with Parking Lots and Parking Garages equipped with License Plate Recognition (LPR) which allows quick automatic payment with no need to look for a payment machine or use cash as it is automatically paid from driver’s account. It saves the driver time and generates more revenue for the Parking Lots and Parking Garages owners as the driver pays for the entire time from entering the parking and leaving it. Payment can happen also by scanning the QR code on the ticket for Parking Lots and Parking Garages without an LPR

PARKING LOCATOR

We believe that serving the driver in the best way is to provide a simple and easy flow that assist the driver locating a parking based on his definition (distance, cost) and availability of the parking places. Our flow does not end with the driver decision where to park, we also assist him with the navigation to the desired parking place and of course also pay for it
